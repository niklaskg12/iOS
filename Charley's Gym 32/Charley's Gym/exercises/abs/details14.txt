Step 1

Lie flat on your back with your hands behind your head and your feet and calves placed flat on a bench. Your knees should be bent.

Step 2

Lift your head and shoulders off of the floor and twist, bringing your right elbow to your left knee. Only your upper body should be moving. Your legs should remain still on the bench. Hold the twist for a second, then return to the starting position.

Step 3

Lift your head and shoulders off of the floor and twist to the right side, bringing your left elbow to your right knee. Hold the twist for a second then return to the starting position. Perform three sets of 20 twist crunches to begin, while working up to more as fitness improves
